Small utility classes developed by third parties are in the edu.lclark.orego.thirdparty package:

  • MersenneTwisterFast, a pseudorandom number generator, is from the ECJ (Evolutionary Computation in Java) project, http://www.cs.gmu.edu/~eclab/projects/ecj/.
  • Gaussian, for computing Gaussian density functions, is from Sedgewick and Wayne, Introduction to Programming in Java.

Both are used with permission.
